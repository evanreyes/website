Hello! My name is Evan Reyes and I am aspiring web developer in Seattle. After spending almost five years as an IT support professional with Apple and Nordstrom, I decided to change careers and pursue web development. Currently, I am working at Axon in IT as I continue to study, build my development skills, network, and hunt for jobs.

I enrolled in Coding Dojo's online bootcamp, where I learned HTML, CSS, Python, and JavaScript, as well as web frameworks that weave together those languages. My primary focus is Python, in particular the Django framework, though I also love JavaScript, the MEAN stack, and React. I am excited and eager to start my career in coding!
